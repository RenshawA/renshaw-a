What is RatTrapTees?

RatTrapTees, or RTT, is a t-shirt designing business I started with a friend in high school. We specialize in making shirts based off iconic basketball moments. We sell these shirts through a print-on-demand website called Redbubble.

On the morning of October 30th, I received a message from Vanessa Macias, girlfriend of NBA superstar Tim Duncan, saying Duncan will be wearing a shirt we designed to teammate Manu Ginobili's jersey retirement!
